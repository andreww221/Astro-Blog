# Performance as a Feature

*Published January 21, 2025*

Performance is not an optimization step; it is a product feature.

> “Users feel performance before they understand features.”

---

## Why Performance Matters

Performance impacts trust, accessibility, and retention.

- Faster sites feel more reliable
- Slow interfaces increase abandonment
- Performance affects SEO rankings

---

## Measuring Performance

```js
console.log("Measure before optimizing");
```

Key metrics include FCP, LCP, and CLS.
